It’s the 1st of December, and the Grinch has struck again. He stole little Oliver’s paper Christmas list and — being extra spiteful this year — encrypted it. He left behind a weird compiled program (a binary) that he claims “contains the secret password.”

Your mission: reverse the binary, extract the password, use it to decrypt the Grinch’s ciphertext, and recover Oliver’s list (which contains the hidden flag).

The format of the flag is ADV{something}
